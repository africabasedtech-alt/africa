Our Mission

Why We Exist

AfricaBased Technologies was founded with a bold vision: to democratize investing for everyday Africans. We believe that wealth creation should not be reserved for the privileged few. Our platform bridges the gap between opportunity and accessibility, enabling anyone with a mobile phone to participate in real investment opportunities across agriculture, technology, real estate, energy, and more.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted and inclusive investment platform, where every individual — regardless of their financial background — can build lasting wealth, gain financial independence, and contribute to the continent's economic growth. We envision a future where millions of Africans are active participants in building the economy, not just spectators.

How It Works

Step 1: Join

Create your free account in under a minute. If someone referred you, use their referral code to join their team — you both benefit.

Step 2: Fund

Deposit funds via M-Pesa (auto or manual). Your deposit balance is ready to invest immediately. Start with any amount you're comfortable with.

Step 3: Invest

Browse investment products across multiple sectors. Each product shows its price, daily return, duration, and ROI. Choose what suits your goals and budget.

Step 4: Earn

Collect your daily returns from the Holdings page. Reinvest your earnings for compound growth, or withdraw to your M-Pesa. It's your money, your choice.

Step 5: Grow

Share your referral link and build your team. As your network invests, you earn commissions automatically. The bigger your team, the more you earn — even while you sleep.
