How it works
  1. Enter your M-Pesa phone number and the amount to deposit.
  2. Click Pay with M-Pesa.
  3. An M-Pesa prompt will appear on your phone asking for your PIN.
  4. Enter your PIN to confirm — your deposit is credited automatically.
